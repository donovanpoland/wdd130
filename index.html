Utah

Utah is a western U.S. state known for its stunning natural landscapes, including five national parks (Zion, Bryce Canyon, Arches, Canyonlands, and Capitol Reef). It has a strong outdoor culture with world-class skiing, hiking, and rock climbing, especially in the Wasatch Mountains. The state has a rich history tied to Native American tribes and the settlement of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, which still influences its culture today. Salt Lake City, the capital, is a hub for business, technology, and outdoor recreation, making Utah a unique blend of modern development and breathtaking wilderness.
