History

Founded in 1998, ©Splash White Water Rafting started as a small passion project by three adventure-loving friends who dreamed of sharing the thrill of river rafting with the world. Nestled in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, our company began with just a single raft, a handful of dedicated guides, and an ambition to provide unforgettable experiences on the water. In the early days, we operated out of a tiny wooden cabin near the Snake River, offering weekend rafting trips to locals and thrill-seekers passing through. Word quickly spread about our expert guides, top-tier safety standards, and, most importantly, the heart-pounding excitement of navigating wild rapids. Within a few years, we expanded our fleet, added multi-day adventure packages, and built a state-of-the-art facility to welcome rafters of all skill levels. Today, ©Splash White Water Rafting is a premier rafting company, offering guided tours on some of the most exhilarating rivers in the country. We've hosted thousands of adventurers, from first-time paddlers to experienced thrill-seekers, all looking to conquer the rapids and create lasting memories.
